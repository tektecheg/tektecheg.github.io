What is RC1?

A programmable kit for building end-to-end STEAM projects in school or at home. It includes built-in input and output capabilities like temperature and light sensors, 8-bit speakers, display screen, output light and input buttons. The kit can be powered through any PC or through an external battery supply.

How learner interact with RC1?

Learners can use block or script programming to program the kit and monitor their work through multiple dashboards.An open-source educational platform for programming and monitoring the TekTech kit. Learner and teacher support

Learner support: MyTekTech is a personalized project library where learners and teachers can get inspired with projects that can have a huge impact on their societies, learning and future. Teachers and learners can also track their progress, share their projects and get answers from the learning community MyTekTech promotes the UN SDG.
Teacher training: Becoming a Maker Educator (BAME) is a teacher boot-camp to introduce teachers to the theoretical backdrop and practical know-hows of maker education. At the end of the five day course, teacher will be able to design and implement their own class plans using the TekTech experience

Technical support

One of the biggest headaches when dealing with technology is maintaining it. Thats why Tektech provides on-demand local technical support to allow schools, teachers and learners to focus on what matters. Our support packages are flexible, affordable and customizable.
