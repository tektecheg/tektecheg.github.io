The TekTech Experience

Combining hardware and software with technical and pedagodgical support to provide institutions and learners with a cutomizable learning journey.

Hardware

An open-source programmable kit for building end-to-end STEAM projects in school or at home. It includes built-in input and output capabilities like temperature and light sensors, 8-bit speakers, display screen, output light and input buttons. The kit can be powered through any PC or through an external battery supply.

Educationl Software

An open-source educational platform for programming and monitoring the TekTech kit. Pedagodgical Support

Learner support: MyTekTech is a personalised project library where learners and teachers can get inspired with projects that can have a huge impact on their socities, learning and future. Teachers and learners can also track their progress, share their projects and get answeres from the learnig commnity MyTekTech promotes the UN SDG.


Teacher training: Becoming a Maker Educator (BAME) is a teacher bootcamp to intriduce teachers to the theoritical backdrop and practical know-hows of maker education. At the end of the five day course, teacher will be able to design and implement their own class plans using the TekTech experience

Technical Support

One of the bigegst headaches when dealing with technology is maintaining it. Thats why Tektech provides on-demand local technical support to allow schools, teachers and learners to focus on what matters. Our support packages are flexible, affordable and customizable.
