Meet TekTech

Tektech is an end-to-end experience for delivering coding and robotics courses. This includes hardware, software, teacher support and technical support.
TekTech kit is an easy-to-use platform for building STEAM projects in school or at home. It provides schools and course providers with a customizable journey for their students and teachers.
