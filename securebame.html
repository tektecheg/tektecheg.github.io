Becoming a maker educator.

B.A.M.E. (Becoming a Maker Educator) is a 8-week program to equip teachers to deliver a unique educational journey using Project-based Learning (PBL) that leverages blended hardware and software approaches.
Teachers will go through a series of workshops covering the latest in educational technology fundamentals and practical application
